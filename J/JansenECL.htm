Jansen, Edouard Constant Louis

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1902, Ngawi, Java. Died: June 24, 1924, Langsa, Atjeh, Sumatra.

 

career:

Overseer, since 1921 in the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service; at first stationed in the teak districts, in 1922 transferred to N. Sumatra, stationed respectively at Langsa, Idi, and Langsa.

 

Collecting localities:

1922. Centr. Java (Sept.): Kedoengdjati, Semarang, Telawa, in teak forest.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO], numbered in the series of Beumée (see there).
