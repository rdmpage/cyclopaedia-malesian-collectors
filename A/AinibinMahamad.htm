Aini bin Haji Mahamad

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

A Forester of the Department of Forestry, Sarawak.

 

Collecting localities:

In 1955 he assisted the forestry member of the Oxford Expedition for three months in the Plieran Valley. He did a small amount of individual collecting too and in that case his name together with that of G.H. Pickles (see there) occurs on the herbarium sheet.
