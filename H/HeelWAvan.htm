Heel, Willem Albert van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1928, Leiden, Z.H., Netherlands.

 

career:

Plant morphologist who attended the University at Leiden (Ph.D. 1966). In 1956 appointed at the Rijksherbarium. He made a study tour to Java, for collecting suitable material for his research.

Author of morphological papers.

 

Collecting localities:

1969. Java (Apr.-Aug.), mainly in Hort. Bog.

 

collections:

Mostly pickled, but also some dried material in Herb. Leiden [L].
