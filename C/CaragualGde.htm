Caragual, Gonzales de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Intendant of the Philippines in the times of la Pérouse (1787) (cf. sub Martinière), sent plant collections from Manila (Luzon) to his mother country,1 probably Spain.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Kotzebue in ‘Entdekkungsreise etc. auf dem Schiffe Rurik, 1815-18’ (Dutch transl., Amsterdam 1822) vol. 3, p.148-149.
